Overview

Designing oxidiser ground support equipment for safe nitrous oxide fill operations.

This project delivers a portable nitrous oxide fill station with pressure control, leak detection and operator safety features.

Role

  • Designing piping, valves and pressure regulation for nitrous oxygen transfer and storage.
  • Integrating safety interlocks, venting systems and instrumentation for controlled fill sequences.
  • Preparing hardware and operating procedures for field use during hybrid rocket campaigns.

Technical focus

Fluid systems

Pressure-controlled flow paths and plumbing suited to cryogenic-style nitrous oxide handling.

Safety systems

Leak detection, emergency isolation and operator control to protect personnel and equipment.

Operational readiness

Field-ready packaging, checklists and commissioning support for worksite deployment.

Process & visuals

Define

Mapped the nitrous oxide fill sequence, pressure control points and safety interlocks for safe operation.

Build

Developed the valve logic, instrumentation panel and printed circuit board layout for automated fills.

Verify

Completed validation checks and performance reviews to ensure the station meets launch support requirements.
