Overview

Building a hydrogen jet test facility for high-speed experimental flow studies.

This project combines safe hydrogen handling with acoustic leak research, supersonic jet delivery and diagnostic readiness for experimental validation.

Role

  • Designing pressure systems, hydrogen safety controls and test cell interfaces for experimental operation.
  • Coordinating instrumentation, flow metering and data acquisition for high-speed diagnostics.
  • Delivering a modular test facility architecture to support iterative study campaigns.

Technical focus

Hydrogen handling

Safe delivery and containment systems for hydrogen feed and combustion test conditions.

Test rig design

Supersonic jet delivery, nozzle mounting and boundary control for experimental repeatability.

Diagnostics

Instrumentation planning and sensor integration for flow, pressure and temperature measurement.
